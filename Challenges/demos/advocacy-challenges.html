A bit of context

In September 2015, all 193 members of the United Nations adopted the Sustainable Development Goals. As a result, we created Givershub out of the question: "How can we raise awareness about the most pressing problems?". The challenge now is to ensure the Goals are implemented in a way that ensures no one is left behind.

Teaching Sustainable Development Goals

The course aims to equip you with relevant information and tools to enable you to take part in the World’s Largest Lesson, to teach people about the SDGs.
